Get Started

Tokuma General Trading is a private limited company and Sister Company of Tokuma Star Business Group.

What began as a small business in 2009 G.C has grown into a diversified and trusted company serving key sectors such as real estate, manufacturing, and general trading. From the very beginning, our focus has been on delivering quality, creating value, and contributing to Ethiopia’s development through hard work and innovation.

Today, Tokuma Star stands as a symbol of growth and commitment, thanks to our dedicated team, strong partnerships, and the trust of our customers. As we continue this journey, we remain focused on expanding our impact while staying true to the values that shaped our success. Thank you for visiting our website and for your interest in Tokuma Star business group. Sincerely,”

The company was established in 2008 G.C engaged in small business activities before it grew to establish today’s capital and subsidiaries. In 2023, the company received a business license under the name of Tokuma Star Business Group and organized four subsidiaries under it.

  • The company imports a wide range of high-quality products to meet local market demands, including industrial equipment, construction materials, electronics, and consumer goods. In addition,
  • Tokuma General Trading actively participates in export activities, supplying agricultural commodities and other Ethiopian products to international markets.
  • With a commitment to excellence, reliability, and sustainable growth, the company strives to build long-term partnerships with clients both locally and globally, contributing to the nation’s economic development through efficient trade practices.

OUR Vision

Tokuma General Trading aspires to become one of Ethiopia’s most prominent and diversified business groups, establishing a strong and sustainable presence across key sectors of the national economy. The company is committed to driving growth through excellence, innovation, and operational integrity. By consistently delivering high-quality products and services, Tokuma General Trading aims to create long-term value for its customers, partners, and communities. With a strong emphasis on social responsibility, the company strives to contribute meaningfully to Ethiopia’s economic development while upholding ethical business practices, empowering employees, and promoting inclusive progress throughout all its ventures.
